Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use The Bibliotech, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: name, email address, and encrypted password
  • Order information: shipping address, order history, and transaction records
  • Usage data: pages visited, books viewed, and cart activity
  • Session data: login sessions and remember-me preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Maintain and secure your account
  • Send order confirmations and shipping updates
  • Send newsletters if you have opted in
  • Improve our website and services

3. Data Security

All passwords are encrypted using BCrypt hashing. We use HTTPS for all data transmission. We do not store full payment card details — all payment processing is handled by secure third-party providers. We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services.

4. Cookies and Sessions

We use session cookies to keep you logged in and remember-me cookies (valid for 30 days) if you choose that option. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can delete cookies at any time through your browser settings.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share data with shipping carriers solely for order fulfillment, and with legal authorities if required by law.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data via your profile page
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of newsletter communications at any time

7. Contact

For any privacy concerns or data requests, please contact us.
